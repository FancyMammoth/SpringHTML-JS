Welcome to Vai.com

Steven Siro Vai (born June 6, 1960) is an American guitarist, songwriter and producer who has sold over 15 million albums. He and his contemporary Joe Satriani are widely regarded as virtuosos on their instruments with their skill extending beyond their affinity for their instrument to their mastery of the technology necessary to present their art to the public.
